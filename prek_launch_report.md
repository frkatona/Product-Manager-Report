
# two-week pre-launch CEO report ("Pre-K Flashcards") 

This report contains a two-week sprint plan and an assessment of discovered launch risks.  With targeted content revisions and stronger accountability across Product, Creative, Marketing, and QA functions, the listing can reach publish-ready status within the sprint window.

## Major Launch Risks & Mitigation

### Risk 1 — Claim Inconsistency
Impact: Listing suppression risk, legal exposure, credibility damage

Mitigation:
- Claim validation gate before creative production
- Centralized specification authority
- Documentation requirement for origin claims

### Risk 2 — SEO Dilution / Keyword Spam
Impact: Reduced indexing quality and conversion rate

Mitigation:
- Assign single SEO owner
- Enforce readability and keyword density standards
- Structured listing review prior to upload

## Listing QA Audit Highlights

### Main Image
- Displays pack size and dimensions
- “Made in USA” claim present
- Acceptable quality but mobile readability risk

### Listing Images
- Pluralization errors present
- Messaging inconsistency regarding origin claim
- Visual hierarchy cluttered

### Title
- Keyword stacking
- Formatting inconsistencies
- Placeholder artifact present (“Learning Material 295”)

### Bullets
- Run-on keyword phrases
- Grammar inconsistencies
- Weak benefit articulation

### A+ Content
- Strong visual tone
- Generic messaging
- Conflicting origin claim

## Process Improvement Recommendation

### Launch Readiness Gate
Require sign-off on:
- Specifications
- Compliance claims
- SEO strategy
- Timeline ownership

---

## QA System Improvement Recommendation

### Structured Listing QA Matrix
Implement ownership tracking, risk scoring, approval workflow, typo detection, and character limit validation within a launch management tool (e.g., Airtable or ClickUp).
